1. **[PLACEHOLDER Aim 1 — Synthesize]** Establish cell-free methods to assemble and reboot infectious phages directly from genome sequence, without a susceptible host.
2. **[PLACEHOLDER Aim 2 — Reconfigure]** Engineer phage host-range and genome modules to rapidly retarget phages against emerging antibiotic-resistant strains.
3. **[PLACEHOLDER Aim 3 — Adaptive framework]** Build a generalizable, on-demand pipeline that shortens the timeline from pathogen identification to a matched, functional phage therapeutic.
